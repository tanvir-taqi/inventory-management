Help

How do I add new iPhone models to the inventory?

To add a new iPhone model to the inventory, navigate to the Inventory Management section and click on the "Add New Model" button. Fill in the required fields, including the model name, description, and initial stock level. Then click "Save" to add the new model to the inventory.

If you need to update the stock level for an existing model, simply navigate to the Inventory Management section, locate the model you want to update, and click on the "Edit" button. You can then update the stock level and click "Save" to update the inventory.

How do I track the inventory levels for each iPhone model?

To view the inventory levels for each iPhone model, navigate to the Inventory Management section and locate the model you want to view. The current stock level will be displayed next to the model name. You can also view the inventory history for the model, including sales and restocks, by clicking on the model name.

If you need to set alerts for low stock levels, navigate to the Alerts section and click on the "Add Alert" button. Choose the iPhone model and the stock level threshold, and click "Save" to set the alert. You will receive an email notification when the stock level falls below the threshold.

How do I generate sales reports?

To generate a sales report, navigate to the Sales Report section and select the desired date range. The report will display the number of iPhone models sold, the revenue generated from the sales, and other related metrics. You can export the report as a CSV file by clicking on the "Export" button.

If you need to view the sales data for a specific iPhone model, click on the model name in the report. This will display the sales history for the model, including the date, quantity sold, and revenue generated.

How do I delete an iPhone model from the inventory?

To delete an iPhone model from the inventory, navigate to the Inventory Management section and locate the model you want to delete. Click on the "Delete" button and confirm the deletion. Note that this action cannot be undone.

How do I adjust the stock level for an iPhone model?

To adjust the stock level for an iPhone model, navigate to the Inventory Management section and locate the model you want to update. Click on the "Edit" button and update the stock level field. You can also add a note to the inventory history for the update. Click "Save" to update the inventory.

How do I view the inventory history for an iPhone model?

To view the inventory history for an iPhone model, navigate to the Inventory Management section and locate the model you want to view. Click on the model name to view the inventory history, which includes sales, restocks, and adjustments to the stock level.

How do I set up email alerts for low stock levels?

To set up email alerts for low stock levels, navigate to the Alerts section and click on the "Add Alert" button. Choose the iPhone model and the stock level threshold, and enter the email address where you want to receive the alerts. Click "Save" to set up the alert.

How do I export the inventory data as a CSV file?

To export the inventory data as a CSV file, navigate to the Inventory Management section and click on the "Export" button. Choose the data range you want to export and click "Export" to download the CSV file. You can then open the file in a spreadsheet program such as Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.
